    Randomizer Rules

    • Ascension is rolled between 0 and 10.
    • Characters are selected randomly for each player, and duplicates are allowed.
    • Modifier count is configurable from 0 to 18 per run, from a total pool of 20. Setting it to 0 rolls a random count based on the modifiers currently enabled.
    • The 3 incompatible starting-deck modifiers are automatically kept mutually exclusive.
